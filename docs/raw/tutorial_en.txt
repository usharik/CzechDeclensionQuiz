📱 HOW TO USE THE APP

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎯 STEP 1: DECLENSION QUIZ

On the main screen you'll see:
• A word at the top (e.g., "pán" - gentleman)
• A set of cards with word forms
• A table with 7 cases (Nominativ, Genitiv, etc.)

WHAT TO DO:
1. Pick up a card with a word form (tap and hold with your finger)
2. Drag it to the correct cell in the table
3. Release your finger - the card will snap into place
4. Fill all 14 cells (7 cases × 2 numbers)
5. Tap the "Check" button ✓

💡 TIP: 
• Left column - singular
• Right column - plural
• Next to each case name you'll see questions (kdo? co? etc.)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

📚 STEP 2: HANDBOOK

Open the menu (☰ top left) → "Handbook"

Here you'll find:
• All declension patterns
• Example words for each type
• Complete declension tables

This is your cheat sheet! Use it when in doubt.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ STEP 3: WORDS WITH ERRORS

The app remembers which words you got wrong!

Open menu → "Words with errors"

Here you can:
• See the list of difficult words
• Practice them again
• Reinforce your learning

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⚙️ STEP 4: SETTINGS

Open menu → "Settings"

You can:
• Choose noun gender (only masculine, feminine, etc.)
• Turn off animation (if it's distracting)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎁 HELPFUL TIPS

✅ Start with simple words (animate masculine)
✅ Don't rush - better slow but correct
✅ Use the handbook - it's not cheating, it's learning!
✅ Review words with errors - that's how you'll remember faster
✅ Practice a little every day - 10 minutes is enough

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I drag a card?
A: Tap the card with your finger, hold and drag to the desired cell

Q: What if the card doesn't snap into place?
A: Make sure you release your finger exactly over the cell

Q: Can I fix a mistake?
A: Yes! Just drag another card to the same spot

Q: How many words are in the app?
A: 14 declension patterns with many examples
